Pizza Recipe

This quick Margherita pizza recipe is a fast and straightforward way to make a classic and flavorful pizza.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature (usually around 475°F/245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Tear fresh mozzarella into pieces and distribute them on top of the sauce.
  5. Place fresh basil leaves on the pizza.
  6. Drizzle with a bit of olive oil.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for about 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
