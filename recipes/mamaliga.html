Mamaliga Recipe

This easy mamaliga recipe provides a quick and simple way to prepare the traditional Romanian cornmeal porridge. Here's a description of the process:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil in a pot.
  2. Once the water is boiling, add 1 teaspoon of salt and stir to dissolve.
  3. Gradually sprinkle the cornmeal into the boiling water, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  4. Continue stirring over medium heat until the mixture thickens to a porridge-like consistency. This usually takes about 5-7 minutes.
  5. If desired, stir in 2 tablespoons of butter for added richness. This step is optional but adds a delicious flavor.
  6. Once the mamaliga reaches the desired thickness, remove it from the heat.
  7. If you want to mold the mamaliga, transfer it to a bowl or mold while it's still hot. Smooth the top with a spatula.
